3.12. Feedback¶

If you have any feedback, data requests, or questions, please post on the SimCenter Regional Testbeds. When creating a New Topic it would be helpful if you could place an indication of your post in the Subject area:

  1. General feedback, e.g., FEEDBACK: Provide comments on the testbed features, documentation, capabilities, etc.

  2. Specific questions, e.g., QUESTION: pose a question for the developer team, cross referencing the stage of the workflow it pertains to such as Hazard Characterization.

  3. Extensions, e.g., EXTENSION: discuss possible extensions of features in current testbeds or new geographical areas where you plan to apply the testbed capabilities.

Note

Community members who wish to contribute to the hurricane testbed are also welcome to express their interest by completing this survey.
