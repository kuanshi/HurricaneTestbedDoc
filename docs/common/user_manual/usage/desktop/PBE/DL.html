DL: Damage and Loss ModelÂ¶

The Damage and Loss panel provides users a convenient way to define the damage and loss model for the building. The dropdown list at the top of the panel allows users to choose between two loss-assessment methods: FEMA P58 [ATC12] and HAZUS MH [FEM18]. The method chosen determines the information displayed in the rest of the panel. The two methods are discussed in the following sections.
